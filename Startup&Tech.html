Custom Chart of Accounts for Startups

Track spending across R&D, marketing, operations, etc., tailored to startup KPIs. Our custom chart of accounts provides detailed visibility into your financial data, making it easier to monitor burn rate, prepare for investor meetings, and identify cost-saving opportunities.

Free Onboarding & Quick Setup

Hassle-free onboarding with first-month setup included. We'll configure your accounting software, integrate with your existing tools, and establish efficient workflows to get your financial systems up and running quickly with minimal disruption to your business operations.

MRR Reporting

Track and analyze your Monthly Recurring Revenue with detailed reports that highlight growth trends, churn rates, and expansion revenue.

Deferred Revenue Management

Fundraising Preparation

Audit / Tax Ready Books

Maintain impeccable financial records that can withstand scrutiny from auditors, investors, and regulatory bodies, with proper documentation and controls.

AR/AP Management

Streamline your accounts receivable and payable processes with automated invoicing, payment tracking, and aging reports to optimize cash flow.

Cash Flow Management

Monitor and forecast your cash position with precision, identifying potential shortfalls before they happen and optimizing your runway.
